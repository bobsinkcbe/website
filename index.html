Meet Prabhu D.A – The Artist Behind BOBS

Art has been a part of my life for as long as I can remember. Ever since childhood, I have been drawn to shapes, symbols, and stories that live inside the imagination. What started as simple sketches on school notebooks slowly transformed into a lifelong passion that now breathes through every tattoo I create.

I am Prabhu D.A, the artist and founder of BOBS — a place where creativity meets precision, and every idea is transformed into meaningful art. Growing up, I knew art wasn’t just something I wanted to do… it was who I was. My pencil was my first tool, and today, the tattoo machine has become the extension of my mind and soul.

For me, tattoos are more than just designs on skin. They are memories, emotions, beliefs, and journeys permanently captured in the form of art. I believe every tattoo has a voice. Whether it’s a powerful symbol, a portrait, a spiritual mark, or a unique custom design that only the wearer understands — I take pride in giving it life with passion and purpose.

At BOBS, every client is treated like a collaborator. I listen, I understand, and then I design with intention. I specialize in styles that connect deeply with people — from fine line and blackwork to detailed illustrative and custom-crafted concepts. My focus is always on quality, hygiene, and originality. No tattoo is rushed, and no story goes unnoticed.

Starting this journey from a young age has shaped not only my skills but also my outlook — art is everywhere, and everyone has a story worth being inked. That belief led me to also build BOBS as a space for learning and growth, offering professional tattoo training for aspiring artists who want to follow their dream.

With continuous practice, exploration, and dedication, I strive every day to evolve as an artist. I learn from people, from life, from culture, and from every masterpiece that walks out the studio door.

If you’re searching for a tattoo that truly represents you, I am here to make your idea become a beautiful reality.

Aftercare Guide

Proper care ensures your tattoo heals beautifully and lasts forever

1

First 24 Hours

  • Keep the bandage on for 2-3 hours
  • Gently wash with unscented soap
  • Pat dry with clean paper towel
  • Apply thin layer of recommended ointment
2

Days 2-7

  • Wash 2-3 times daily with gentle soap
  • Continue applying ointment sparingly
  • Avoid submerging in water
  • Don't pick at scabs or peeling skin
3

Week 2-4

  • Switch to unscented lotion
  • Continue gentle washing routine
  • Avoid direct sunlight
  • Your tattoo should be fully healed
